## 大括号

```
$$ F^{HLLC}=\left\{
\begin{array}{rcl}
F_L       &      & {0      <      S_L}\\
F^* _ L     &      & {S_L \leq 0 < S_M}\\
F^* _ R     &      & {S_M \leq 0 < S_R}\\
F_R       &      & {S_R \leq 0}
\end{array} \right. $$

```
$$ F^{HLLC}=\left\{
\begin{array}{rcl}
F_L       &      & {0      <      S_L}\\
